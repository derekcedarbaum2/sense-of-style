## Core Voice

**State things as `<facts | hypotheses | options>`.** `<Describe how you handle certainty. Do you hedge? Avoid hedging? Distinguish between the two explicitly?>`

**`<First-principles | concrete-example | top-down>`-led.** `<How do you build an argument? Trace to root causes first? Lead with the conclusion? Show the data and let the reader infer?>`

**Assumes `<competence | a learning curve | mixed>` in the reader.** `<Do you use domain shorthand without explanation, or do you over-define? When is domain jargon OK?>`

**`<Profanity | none>`.** `<If you use it, when? For emphasis? Anger? Both? If not, just say so — sense-of-style will not insert profanity unprompted but will preserve yours if you use it.>`

**Tribal vs. independent.** `<Do you stake out predictable positions, or call balls and strikes? This is mostly relevant for political and social writing — but it shapes how you frame disagreement everywhere.>`
